# Army Roadmap

Tracks implementation status of all Warhammer 40,000 10th Edition faction rules and detachments.

**Legend:** `[ ]` = not started · `[~]` = in progress · `[x]` = complete

---

## Imperium

### Space Marines

**Faction Rule: Oath of Moment**
At the start of your Command phase, select one enemy unit. Until the start of your next Command phase, each time a Space Marines model attacks that unit, you can re-roll the Hit roll and the Wound roll.

- [ ] Faction rule implemented
- [ ] Faction rule tested

**Detachments:**

- [ ] **Gladius Task Force** — *Combat Doctrines:* At the start of your Command phase, select one doctrine: Devastator (re-roll Hit rolls of 1 for ranged), Tactical (units that Fall Back can still shoot and/or charge), or Assault (+1 Strength to melee on a turn you charged).
- [ ] **Anvil Siege Force** — *Shield of the Imperium:* Attacks targeting units within your deployment zone or that haven't moved this turn get -1 to Wound roll.
- [ ] **Ironstorm Spearhead** — *Armoured Wrath:* VEHICLE/WALKER ranged attacks get +1 to Hit if Remained Stationary.
- [ ] **Firestorm Assault Force** — *Close-range Eradication:* All ranged weapons gain [ASSAULT]. Ranged attacks within 12" get AP improved by 1.
- [ ] **Vanguard Spearhead** — *Shadow Masters:* Units can only be targeted by ranged attacks from within 12" (except TITANIC).
- [ ] **1st Company Task Force** — *Extremis-level Threat:* Terminator models get +1 to Hit when targeting CHARACTER, MONSTER, or VEHICLE.
- [ ] **Stormlance Task Force** — *Lightning War:* +1 to Hit on turns the unit made a Normal move or Advanced. +1 to Wound on turns the unit charged.
- [ ] **Inner Circle Task Force** — *Righteous Vanguard:* Melee attacks re-roll Hit rolls of 1 and Wound rolls of 1 while within range of an objective marker.
